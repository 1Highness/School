About Our School

Bishop Agori Iwe Memorial Anglican Boys' Grammar School is a prestigious institution committed to academic excellence, discipline, and moral upbringing. Established to nurture young boys into responsible, God-fearing leaders, our school stands as a beacon of hope and transformation in Ughelli, Delta State.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a model secondary school known for raising upright and intelligent leaders.

Mission: To provide balanced education that combines academic excellence with moral and spiritual development.
